About Me


My name is Lucy Marie Lesire, I am 23 years old and work as a Game Developer at AlterEyes.

At the young age of 4, I was introduced to gaming by my older brothers, who showed me how to play "Tekken 2". 19 years later I am a passionate gamer and owner of a bachelors degree in Game Development at Digital Arts & Entertainment.

My favourite type of games are MOBA's and First Person Shooters. However during my studies I have come to love Indie games and the beauty that they bring.
Other than Games, I also enjoy programming, I have been a programmer since high school, where I studied Electronics. My main coding languages are C++ and C#. I use Unreal Engine (4/5) and Unity.
