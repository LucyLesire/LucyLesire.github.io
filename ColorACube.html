Color a Cube

Color a Cube is an immersive coloring experience that lets you explore and bring voxel worlds to life, one cube at a time.

The game has been released in early access on Meta Horizon. It has been developed by AlterEyes, where I worked as a programmer.

For this project I also worked on multiple packages.


My Role in Color a Cube

My role can be divided into 2 projects, an abstract store package I wrote to handle the game's economy and my work on the game itself.
