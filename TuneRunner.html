Tune Runner

During one of my courses, Game Mechanics, we had to make a game around 2 keywords "Melody" and "Countdown".
After some searching for some ideas, I came up with a music beat sync game where the goal is the move on the beat, whilest making sure you don't run out of time.

This is ofcourse only a prototype so the art assets are rather basic, and the "beat calculator" is not quite perfect. However it does already produce quite a nice prototype.
Your goal in the game is to hit the beats (on the music), by pressing space when running over them with the player. In the meanwhile a countdown clock ticks down, and it is your goal to not make it go to 0. During the game you can gain time by hitting beats, and lose time by missing beats and hitting obstacles.

The Unreal Engine 4.27

We had to work in the Unreal Engine because the goal of this course was making us familiar with The Unreal Engine and C++ in Unreal.

My goal was ofcourse to have a full prototype around my two keywords, in order to achieve this I implemented:

  • Procedural spawning of beats, that are spawned upon beats of the music
  • Turning music and sound waves into beats.
  • Collision detections between player and beats.
  • Implementing of powerups that slow down and speed up time.
  • Implementing powerups that allow you to move on all lanes at the same time and automatically activate beats
  • Simple movement between lanes.
  • Music that plays through the game.
  • HUD that shows countdown clock.

Learning

During this Project I have learned how to make a prototype in Unreal Engine.
I have also learned more about beat syncing and how to use soundwaves to turn them into beats.
Furthermore I leanred how to use (simple) procedural spawning, which helped me a lot in later projects. Finally I also learned how to write c++ in Unreal Engine.
