Miss Misfortune

During the Fall holiday of 2021, me and my friend Nathan (Artist) decided to join a gamejam hosted by students at DAE, the theme of the jam was "Skull".
After some brainstorming we decided to make a 2D platformer, this would make the load on us not too difficult, because we only had 2 days.

The goal of the game is to pass all 13 levels, either containing parcour or a form of misfortune that the player has to cause.

Some types of misfortune can include: "Tipping over salt, breaking mirrors, walking under ladders, ..."

Learning

During this project I have learned how to make a full fledged 2D game in Unity.
Furthermore I have also learned how to work under a time pressure, since we only had 3 days to complete the project.
I learned how to write 2D movement behaviors, 2D interactions and 2D level design.
Finally I have also learned how to implement sound in Unity.

The Unity Engine

We chose the Unity Engine because it works perfectly for developing 2D games.

My role in the project was very broad, due to the small team size, I had to take all the coding on me. My work contained the following:

  • Writing and implementing the movement behavior.
  • Writing and implementing the misfortune challenges.
  • Writing and implementing the death mechanic.
  • Writing and implementing a score system, that keeps track of howmany challenges you completed, aswel as skulls collected.
  • Implementing the skull movement.
  • Implementing the HUD & menu's
  • Implementing the sound that activate on certain actions, music that plays throughout the game.
