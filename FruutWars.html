Fruut Wars

In one of our courses, Game Projects, we were assigned a group of 2 programmers and 3 artists with the goal of making a vertical slice of a couch game.
We chose to make a Couch PVP shooter, in Unreal Engine 4.27 where you play as fruits and vegetables.

The goal of the game is to kill eachother by firing fruits & vegetables, pick up better weapons and avoid falling in the water.

Learning

During this course I have learned how to use C++ with Unreal Engine.
Furthermore I have also learned how to work on a big project with a (relativly) big team.

Finally I have learned how to create and setup a local multiplayer game in unreal.

Unreal Engine 4

We chose Unreal Engine 4 because of it's built in multiplayer features, particle system and blueprint functionality. We wrote most of our functionallity in C++ and some minor alterations in blueprints.
