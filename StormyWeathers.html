Stormy Weathers

During Creaweek at DAE, we could participate in a GameJam. Me and some friend (2 Programmer & 2 Artists) joined. We had 4 days to make a game around the theme "Peace and Chaos".
We chose to make a Chaotic flying game where you have to fly through Peaceful areas and Chaotic areas.

The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible and getting a good score. However you can die by being hit by mines, ufo's, lightning, ... .

Learning

During this Project I have learned how to make a full fledged game in Unity.
Furthermore I have also learned how to work under a time pressure, since we only had 4 days to complete the project.
Also I learned how to write realstic movement for planes.
Finally I have also learned how to use procedural spawning, managing and deleting of objects.

The Unity Engine

We chose the Unity Engine because we only had 4 days and Unity allowed us to jump right into the action of creating our game.

My role in our group has been very wide, since we are only with 2 programmers, I had to take half of the work on me. My work contained the following:

  • Writing and Implementing the "Mine areas"
  • Writing and implementing the "Peaceful area", 'healthpacks" and "slow/speed rings.
  • Writing the procedural spawning, managing and deleting of terrain, clouds, "Chaotic areas" and "Peaceful areas".
  • Writing and implementing "The Dead zone", which makes sure that you can't leave the playable area
  • Partly Implementing the HUD & menu's
  • Helping my other programmer with their tasks (Plane movement, UFO spawning, UFO AI, Lightning and Animations)
