Big Shots


Co-op VR Mech Game!





Big Shots VR

Big Shots is an action-packed VR Roguelite where you play as a Big Mech, stomping on alien bugs.

The game has been released on PlayStation VR2, Meta VR, PICO XR, SideQuest and Steam. The game has been developed by AlterEyes, where I worked as a programmer.

My Role in Big Shots

  • All enemy behaviours
  • Custom A* Point Graphs for crawling and flying enemies.
  • Custom Behaviour Tree and Blackboard systems
  • Enemy difficulty scaling, modifiers and upgrades
  • PlayStation requirements for PSVR2
  • Meta requirements for Quest 2 & 3
  • Unity Cloud Save- and Friends systems
  • Photon Fusion Networking
  • Navmesh Generation Tool
  • Wave Generation Tool
  • Cheat Menu
