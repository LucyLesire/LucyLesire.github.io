Excape

During one of my courses, Game Mechanics, we had to make a game around 2 keywords "Exile" and "Dismember".
After some searching for some ideas, I came up with a survival game where the goal is the dismember your enemies.

This is ofcourse only a prototype so the dismembering is just done by removing limbs (arms & legs). The player can dismember those limbs by either shooting them with a laser or attacking them with an axe.

Learning

During this Project I have learned how to make a prototype in Unity.

I have also learned more about raycasts in this project, allowing me to fully understand them.

Furthermore I leanred how to use (simple) procedural spawning around the player, which helped me a lot in later projects.

The Unity Engine

We had to work in the Unity Engine because the goal of this course was making us familiar with The Unity Engine and C#.

My goal was ofcourse to have a full prototype around my two keywords, in order to achieve this I implemented:

  • Procedural spawning of enemies in waves, with increased difficulty per wave. Enemies will only spawn to the closest spawn points around you.
  • Limbs that have health and dissapear on 0HP.
  • A Laser gun that uses raycasts to calculate where it is shooting.
  • An axe that uses raycast to calculate if you hit a limb.
  • Pickups that allow you to increase health, ammo and open doors (using raycasts).
  • Sounds that play on certain events, aswel as music that plays throughout the game. Both can be turned up or down using the settings menu
  • HUD that shows ammo and health.
