About me

My name is Lucy Marie Lesire, I am 21 years old and I am currently studying Digital Arts and Entertainment at Howest Kortrijk. I am starting my internship in February of 2023 and will graduate at in June 2023.
I have been playing games since I was 4 years old and always had a strong bond with games. In the Summer of 2018, I decided that I wanted to see the process of how games were made and what I could do to make them myself.
The folling year, in the summer of 2019, I decided to join DAE and I have been working on improving my skill at devloping games ever since. My favourite types of games are MOBA's and FPS (Mainly League of Legends and Valorant), however during my studies I have come to love Indie games and the beauty that they bring. Games like Stardew Valley, The Witness, Celeste, Life is strange, ... exactly the type of games I want to be making in the now and future.
Other then Games, I also enjoy programming, I have been a programmer since high school, where I studied Electronics. My main coding language is C++, but I also use C#. My preffered engine is Unity, however I also use Unreal.
